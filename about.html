A Gamer Who Loves Life

 

I grow up with all kinds of E-games. I really appreciate company from games in my life. At the same time, I get to know a lot of great friends who share the same interests. Great old times!

 

I really hope that games can be accepted widely and can be treated seriously. I am sad that I can't be a professional gamer due to realistic reasons. However, I respect every professional player who devout their time to their favorate games as a professional. At the same time, I admire their courage and determination, together with their love toward games. I watch game series with my friends, just as we watch a tennis match or a basketball match.

 

I would like to continue my study in Computer Science to know more about programming and how to make games. It's late for me to find my true love as future career but hopefully it is not too late. I believe that, one day, I can make great games that everyone can enjoy.
